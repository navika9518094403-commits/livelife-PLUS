Our Story

Why I Built LL+

I looked around and saw so many people — including myself — frustrated, tired, and stuck. Not because they were bad people. Just because of small bad habits that quietly piled up over time.

I realised: if we could just swap those habits for better ones, everything changes. Sleep. Focus. Happiness. Confidence. Energy. It all connects.

So I built LL+ — a free AI-powered tool to help anyone, anywhere, understand their lifestyle and take real steps toward a better one. No judgment. No cost. Just change.
